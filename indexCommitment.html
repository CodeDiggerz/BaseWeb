Potatotiser is a vision of four young minds who are trying to improve the situation of farmers in country . Thats where potatotisation came in the picture .
We are helping scientists and technocrats are bigger audience and helping farmers by providing them the newest Technologies which they were not getting .
Our only purpose is that no farmer commits suicide and the hard work and reasearch of any scientist never gets wasted .
